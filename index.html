How To Play

The goal of the game, "Cards of War" is to defeat the House in a classic showdown of random luck.

To start, click on the "Start A New Deck" button. This will generate a new deck behind the scenes and activate the "Draw Card" button at the bottom.

The game ends when the cards remaining reach zero and either you or the House gets a winning message. If you want to try again, simply generate a new deck and draw another card.

When you're ready, start a new deck and click on the "Draw Card" button to test your luck! 🍀
